Hello! I'm Andi, a product designer currently based in Chicago. I've been lucky to have lived in many diverse places around the world and my approach to design comes from that experience. There are a lot of different opinions out there and it's important to understand their context. It's the best part about this field - I get to spend a lot of time talking to people about the way they view the world and use that knowledge to make something useful.

I'm always looking for new ways to learn more about the world and strike a balance between culture and design. Let's chat.
