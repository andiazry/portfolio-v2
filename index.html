About

Hello.I'm a product designer who is currently based in Chicago. I graduated from the Academy of Art University with a BFA Architecture degree and I'm always looking for new ways to approach and solve design challenges. My philosophy in design stems from my background of living in various countries around the world - I believe that design should be inclusive, flexible and culturally relevant. Design should take into account not only aesthetic principles but also social principles - aware of its context and affect on society at large. Only when there is a balance between the user, culture and design can challenges be overcome.
